Network Engineering

A network is a collection of interconnected nodes or devices that are capable of sharing data and resources with each other. In the context of computing, a network can refer to a series of interconnected computers, servers, and other devices that can communicate with each other to share information and resources. Networks can be local, such as a home or office network, or they can be wide-reaching, such as the internet.
